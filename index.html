About Us

Built on Quality Workmanship and Attention to Detail

Prestige Surface Solutions was founded to bring a higher standard to exterior cleaning and restoration — one where every roof, driveway and façade is treated with the same care as the homes and businesses we work on. Our team uses professional-grade equipment and proven techniques across soft washing, pressure washing and surface restoration, so results last rather than just looking good for a week.

We proudly serve homeowners, commercial property owners, property managers, real estate agents, builders and body corporates throughout Sydney and the Greater Sydney Region. Whether it's a one-off restoration job or an ongoing property maintenance plan, our goal is simple: protect your property's value and make it look its best.
