Soft Washing in Sydney

Not every surface can handle high pressure. Render, stucco, painted weatherboards and timber decking need a gentler touch — our soft washing service uses low-pressure application and surface-safe treatments to lift dirt and grime without causing damage.

Soft Washing Services

  • House siding
  • Stucco and render
  • Painted exterior walls
  • Vinyl siding
  • Exterior timber

Why It Matters

High pressure on soft or aged surfaces can strip paint, force water behind cladding, or splinter timber. Soft washing achieves the same clean result while protecting the integrity of the surface underneath.
